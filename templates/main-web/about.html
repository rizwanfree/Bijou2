About Us

Welcome to The Bijou LLC, a premier property management company based in Detroit. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we have proudly served our community and hosted thousands of satisfied customers. Our portfolio includes multiple properties across Detroit, Pontiac, and Gross Pointe, offering a range of fully furnished houses and rooms for rent.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At The Bijou, we understand the diverse needs of our tenants, which is why we provide flexible leasing options, including both short-term and long-term leases. Our commitment to excellence drives us to continually expand our offerings by acquiring new properties and transforming them into luxurious homes that meet the highest standards.

Exceptional Guest Experience

We prioritize delivering an exceptional experience for our guests. Our dedicated team of experts works tirelessly around the clock to ensure that our services exceed expectations and maintain the quality our customers deserve. Located in the heart of Detroit's city center, our properties are ideally situated to provide easy access to local amenities and attractions.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a thriving community where individuals experience exceptional living standards in luxurious accommodations, enhancing their quality of life and fostering personal and professional growth.

Mission

To provide high-quality, fully furnished rental properties that cater to the diverse needs of our tenants, offering both short-term and long-term leasing options. We are committed to expanding our portfolio by acquiring and transforming properties into luxury homes, ensuring our guests receive the best experience possible through dedicated service and expert management.
