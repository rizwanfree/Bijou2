The Bijou LLC Privacy Policy

Introduction

At The Bijou LLC, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our property management services, including our website and SMS communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Communication Data: including SMS consent information and messages exchanged with us.
  • Usage Data: information about how you use our website and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage our property management services.
  • Communicate with you via SMS, email, or phone regarding appointments, promotions, billing, and updates.
  • Improve our services and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

The Bijou LLC respects your privacy and does not sell or rent your personal information. Specifically:

  • SMS opt-in and phone numbers collected for SMS communication purposes will not be shared with any third party or affiliates for marketing purposes.
  • We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying "STOP" or contacting us directly.
  • You may update or correct your personal information by contacting us.
  • You have the right to request access to the information we hold about you.

6. Accessibility of This Policy

This Privacy Policy is available on a single page and is linked in the footer of every page on our website for easy access.
